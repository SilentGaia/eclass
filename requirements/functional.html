2. Functional Requirements

The primary functionality of E-Classroom is to improve communication and interaction between teacher and students for lectures, and to better organize lectures for easy note-taking for all. Every student, with their assigned roles and permissions, will be able to use E-Classroom to communicate their thoughts to others and take notes on their respective lecture slides.

Following an overview of the E-Classroom user interface , details of system use are presented in the following scenarios:

  • an instructor preparing a lecture
  • an instructor presenting a lecture
  • an individual viewing a presentation
  • an individual utilizing drawing and canvas tools
  • an instructor managing the roster
  • an individual interacting with students and instructor via chat
  • an individual viewing lectures remotely and offline
  • an individual navigating through and using the taskbar
These scenarios cover all functional requirements for the system.
