2.5.2.3. Managing Layer Visibility

When a new layer is made, the default visibility is for visible. To turn off the layer visibility, click on the open eye next to the layer you want to turn off as shown in Figure 5.

Figure 5: Main view.


Once the eye is clicked on, the eye will appear closed as shown in Figure 6. Now the layer you closed the eye on will not be seen on the powerpoint.
