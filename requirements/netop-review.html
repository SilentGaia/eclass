1.5.1. Netop

Netop is a classroom management software. It is mainly a tool geared towards teachers teaching in an electronic environment. It has functionalities for teacher interaction with teachers among an electronics set up. It is generally used for computer labs with some setup of computers, with one machine being designated administrator rights.

The Netop classroom management would be good to look at for teach to student interactions.

Good Features:

  • Able to showcase student's screens, displaying their work for others
  • Able to share instructors screen with students
  • Dynamic screen annotating, allows teacher to mark up their presentations as they give them
  • Allows chatting between instructor and students

Bad Features:

  • Sharing instructor's screen isn't interactable - it locks keyboard and mouse for student
  • Lots of control over the student. Instructor is allowed to manage internet use, applications, computer functions, etc

Missing Features:

  • While the teacher is allowed to share screens between students and themselves, they arenâ€™t able to give students control over the presentation
  • Annotating is only for the teacher. Students are not able to interact with the presentation the teacher is giving.
  • No layering over the presentations. If the teacher is annotating their presentation, the ink cannot be adjusted to become see through, and a blank slide cannot be put over the current one.
